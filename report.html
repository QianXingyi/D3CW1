2.Multiple Representation

In this dataset, for the country column, the developer used the text facet in Open Refine to list all the country code and then used text facet of the country_long column to check the countries’ full names. And then found the representation problems:

(1) N-KOR is used to represent North Korea. However, most of the data in the dataset used PRK to represent North Korea. So, used PRK to replace it.

(2) S-KOR is used to represent South Korea. However, most of the data in the dataset used KOR to represent South Korea. So, used KOR to represent them.

(3) CH is used to represent Chile. However, most of the data used CHL to represent Chile.

(4) NVNM and SVNM are used to represent North Vietnam and South Vietnam. However, these two countries are becoming a whole of Vietnam, and used VNM to represent, and also used Vietnam to replace North Vietnam and South Vietnam.

(5) USSR was representing The Soviet Union. Now it no longer exists, the developer used Russia’s code RUS to represent it. And used Russia to replace USSR in the country_long column.

(6) AU is used to represent Austria. However, AUT is used by most of the data. So, used AUT to replace the AU.

(7) AZ is used to represent Azerbaijan. However, AZE is used by most of the data. So, used AZE to replace the AZ.

(8) ZAF is are used to represent South Africa. However, SA is used by most of the data. So, used SA to replace the ZAF.

(9) NLD is used to represent New Zealand. However, NZL is used by most of the data. So, used NZL to replace it.

(10) UK is used to represent the United Kingdom, However, most of the data used GBR. So, used GBR to replace the UK.

(11) kor need to be capitalized to mean South Korea

(12) sA needs to be capitalized to mean South Africa.

(13) RHD (Rhodesia) is now named Zimbabwe, used ZWE (Zimbabwe) in the dataset to replace it.

(4) London is used as the country_long, use the United Kingdom to replace it.

7.Wrong Format

The developer chooses to remove redundant elements from the data and overwrite the format of the data to complete the correction of wrong format.

(1) While cleaning the data, the developer found that the estimated_generation_gwh column has some columns contain characters (gwh). The developer used the command value.replace(“(gwh)”,””) to delete the characters. And used Numeric facet to change the values to numbers.

(2) While cleaning the data of the commissioning_year column. Found some year with month, the developer deleted the months, they are 1988.1, 1993.5,1955.6,1997.4,1998.3,1999.5,1999.1,2002.5.

(3) And the columns of commissioning_year also have some values like 1477, 15123 and 1774. The developer thinks these means 7/7/2014 and 2/12/2015. And changed them into 2014 and 2015.
